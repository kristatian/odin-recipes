Italian Wedding Soup!

Description

A beautiful recipe from my co-works wedding. We didn't have this soup but I did get it afterwards from Swiss Chalet. It contains a rich broth mixed with some meatballs, spinach, and pasta. Perfect for a cold winters night or, it can be made for a light summer evening.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound extra-lean ground beef
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 0.25 cup dried bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • 3 tablespoons minced onion
  • 2.5 quarts chicken broth
  • 2 cups spinach, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup seashell pasta
  • 0.75 cup diced carrots

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine beef, egg, bread crumbs, cheese, basil, and onion. Shape mixture into 3/4-inch balls and set aside.
  2. In a large stockpot, heat chicken broth to boiling. Stir in spinach, pasta, carrot, and meatballs.
  3. Return to a boil and then reduce the meat to medium.
  4. Cook, stirring frequently, at a slow boil for 10 minutes or until pasta is al dente and meatballs are no longer pink inside.
  5. Serve hot with Parmesan cheese sprinkled on top.
